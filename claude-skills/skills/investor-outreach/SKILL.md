# Investor Outreach Skill

## Outreach Tiers

```
Tier 1: Warm Intro (highest conversion)
  → Request intro via mutual connection
  → LinkedIn 2nd-degree connections
  → Portfolio founder referrals

Tier 2: Targeted Cold Email
  → Investor has written about your space
  → Recent investment in adjacent company
  → Spoke at conference about your problem

Tier 3: Form/LP (lowest priority)
  → Website "pitch us" forms
  → AngelList / Visible profiles
```

## Cold Email Template

```
Subject: [Specific hook — their portfolio, their writing, or shared connection]

Hi [First name],

[1 sentence connecting to their work: "Saw your investment in X and your post about Y..."]

[Company] is [one-line pitch]. We've [traction metric] in [timeframe].

Would love to share more — do you have 20 minutes in the next two weeks?

[Name]
[Role] | [Company]
[calendly link]
```

**Length**: 5-7 sentences. No attachments in cold email.

## Warm Intro Request

```
Subject: Intro request — [Your Company] → [Target Investor]

Hey [Connector's name],

Would you be open to an intro to [Investor] at [Firm]?

We're raising a [round] for [Company]. [One sentence traction].
They've invested in [similar company] and would be a great fit.

I've attached a brief forwardable blurb below if it helps.

---
[Forwardable blurb: 3 sentences — what you do, traction, the ask]
```

## Follow-Up Sequence

```
Email 1: Initial outreach (Day 0)
Email 2: Follow-up (Day 7 — new data point if possible)
Email 3: Final follow-up (Day 21 — "closing the loop")

After 3 emails with no response: mark as inactive
```

## Meeting Prep

- Research the investor's last 5 investments
- Know their thesis and stage focus
- Prepare 3 questions for them (shows you've done homework)
- Have 30-second and 2-minute version of your pitch ready
- Know your numbers cold: ARR, growth, burn, runway
